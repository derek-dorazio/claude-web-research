# Australia Trip — Restaurant Recommendations

## Topic
Find the top 6 restaurant recommendations for each location on an upcoming trip to Australia.

## Locations
1. Melbourne — CBD area
2. Hobart, Tasmania
3. Swansea, Tasmania
4. Uluru (Ayers Rock area)
5. Port Douglas, Queensland
6. Hamilton Island, Queensland
7. Sydney — waterfront area

## Criteria
- **Price range**: Mid-level ($$$ classification, not fine dining but not cheap eats)
- **Views preferred**: Waterfront, ocean view, or other great scenic views when possible
- **Cuisine variety**: Mix of ethnicities per location, especially local Australian or authentic regional cuisine
- **Exclusions**: No steakhouses, no Italian restaurants (can get those at home)
- **Authenticity**: Prefer locally owned, authentic cuisine over chain restaurants

## Questions to Answer
- What are the 6 best mid-range restaurants in each location?
- Which restaurants have the best views or waterfront seating?
- What cuisine types are represented (seafood, Asian, Australian modern, etc.)?
- Are reservations required or recommended?
- What are the standout signature dishes at each?
- Any seasonal considerations or hours to be aware of?

## Desired Output Format
For each location, provide a table or list with:
- Restaurant name
- Cuisine type
- Price range indicator
- View/ambiance notes
- Must-try dish
- Reservation info
- Brief description (1-2 sentences)
