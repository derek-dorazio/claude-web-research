# Skill: Compare & Contrast

Produce a structured comparison of multiple options, tools, approaches, or concepts.

## When to Use

Apply this skill when research involves choosing between alternatives, benchmarking products, or understanding trade-offs between approaches.

## Instructions

1. Identify the items being compared
2. Define comparison criteria relevant to the topic
3. Research each item against every criterion
4. Build a comparison matrix
5. Provide a synthesis with recommendations or observations

## Output Format

```markdown
## Comparison: {{TOPIC}}

### Items Compared
1. <Item A>
2. <Item B>
3. <Item C>

### Comparison Matrix

| Criteria | Item A | Item B | Item C |
|---|---|---|---|
| <criterion 1> | <finding> | <finding> | <finding> |
| <criterion 2> | <finding> | <finding> | <finding> |
| <criterion 3> | <finding> | <finding> | <finding> |

### Strengths & Weaknesses

**Item A**
- Strengths: ...
- Weaknesses: ...

**Item B**
- Strengths: ...
- Weaknesses: ...

### Synthesis
<Overall observations, patterns, and context-dependent recommendations>
```

## Placeholder

- `{{TOPIC}}` — What is being compared
- `{{INPUT}}` — Specific items or criteria to include
